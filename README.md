# Bike-Store
The goal of this project was to understand how data can help businesses optimize operations, improve decision-making, and increase profitability.
• What this project focuses on:
 - Analyzing bike sales data;
 - Exploring product performance;
 - Understanding customer purchasing patterns;
 - Identifying trends that impact business growth.
• What I worked on:
 - Data exploration and cleaning;
 - Building analytical queries and insights;
 - Creating structured analysis to understand sales patterns;
 - Interpreting the results from a business perspective;
• How this project can help a business:
 - Identify best-selling products and optimize inventory;
 - Understand customer buying behavior;
 - Improve sales strategies and marketing decisions;
 - Track overall store performance and growth trends.
• What I learned from this project:
 - How to work with real-world business data;
 - Transforming raw data into actionable insights;
 - Thinking about problems from a business and analytical perspective;
 - Improving my skills in data analysis, problem solving, and decision-making;
Projects like this help me move closer to my goal of becoming a Data Analyst / Data Scientist, where data is used to solve real business problems.
